Our Story

Three generations.
One standard.

Mark Smith didn't start a construction company — he continued a family tradition. His son Austin is doing the same thing. This is what that looks like when it's real.

"Most builders start with a license. Mark Smith started with his grandfather's tools."

What started as watching and learning on job sites as a teenager became a 40-year career — and then a company. Rainier Custom Homes was built on the premise that a builder who cares about every nail, every frame, and every client relationship is worth waiting for.

Mark Smith

Founder & General Contractor

Mark Smith grew up around construction. His grandfather XXXXX had been a builder before him, and Mark spent his teenage years watching, learning, and eventually doing — on job sites around XXXXX Florida.

After years working across residential and light commercial construction, Mark founded Rainier Custom Homes in XXXXX, building the company around three things he'd seen too many builders get wrong: transparency, craftsmanship, and actually returning a phone call.

Austin Smith

Operations Director

Austin Smith didn't choose construction by accident. He grew up on job sites with his father, and by the time he was old enough to drive, he already understood how a house came together — from the foundation pour to the final trim.

Austin went on to earn a degree in Business Management from Lynn University in Boca Raton, bringing a formal understanding of operations, client management, and business development back to the family company. That combination — field experience from childhood, plus a business education — is what makes Austin effective.

Today Austin runs the day-to-day of Rainier Custom Homes: client communication, subcontractor scheduling, site management, and project coordination. When you work with Rainier, Austin is your primary point of contact — and he will always be reachable.
