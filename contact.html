Common Questions

What most people want to know first.

Do I need to already own a lot?

No. We work with clients at every stage — if you have a lot, we can start planning immediately. If you're still searching, Austin has relationships with agents across Central Florida who know which lots are build-ready and what they can realistically support.

How many homes do you take on at once?

We intentionally limit our active builds to what we can oversee properly. We don't take on more work than we can manage at a high standard. If our pipeline is full, we'll tell you honestly and discuss timing.

What's the minimum budget you work with?

Our custom homes typically start around $500K all-in, depending on the market and scope. Investor subdivision homes can come in lower. The best first step is a real conversation about what you're trying to build.

Do you work with investors?

Selectively, yes. Investors who bring us lots or teardowns in markets we already work in are a good fit. We're not looking for wholesalers or flip timelines — we're looking for investors who want quality builds and long-term relationships.

How long does a custom home take?

From permit approval to completion, most custom homes run XXXXX months depending on scope and municipality. We'll give you a realistic timeline at the start — and we hold to it.

Who will I be talking to day-to-day?

Austin Smith handles all client communication and project management. You'll have his direct number and email from day one. Mark is on every job site, but Austin is your primary point of contact — and he's reachable.
