The Rainier Story

"Most builders start with a license.
Mark Smith started with his
grandfather's tools."

Charles Rainier built homes the way people used to — with patience, precision, and pride in what they left behind. His grandson Mark grew up on those job sites, learning the craft before he ever opened a textbook. That foundation led him to the University of Miami on a football scholarship, a business degree, and an MBA from Florida Tech — and then straight back to construction. Not because he had to. Because nothing else was worth doing at that level.

Mark spent decades building that standard into every project. Then, just like his grandfather did for him, he brought his son Austin into the business — not to hand him a title, but to teach him the craft from the ground up. Austin grew up on these job sites. He earned his Business Management degree from Lynn University in Boca Raton, then came back to the work he'd always known. Today he runs the day-to-day — the projects, the timelines, the client relationships — carrying forward everything Mark built, and raising the bar on it.

Three generations. One standard. That's not a tagline. That's the only way we know how to work.
