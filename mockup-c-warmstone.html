The Rainier Story

"Most builders start with a license.
Mark Smith started with his
grandfather's tools."

Charles Rainier built homes the way people used to — with patience, precision, and pride in what they left behind. His grandson Mark grew up watching, then working, then understanding. That obsession led him to the University of Miami on a football scholarship, a business degree, and an MBA — and then back to construction. Not because he had to. Because nothing else was worth doing at that level.

Today, Mark's son Austin runs projects alongside him. Three generations of people who chose building when they could have chosen anything else. That's not a selling point. That's just who we are.
